Titles

these are the styles that you can apply to different titles.

  • H1 titles, normally lobster, all caps and with a font-size of 35px - 40px, letter spacing of 4px-5px, underlined

  • H2, normally Anton, with a font-size of 25px - 35px, underlined or with a dashed border, depending the situation.

  • H3 and lower style, Lexend Peta, sizing dependingon the situation

  • Normal paragraphs are Lexend Peta, sizing dependant on the information, on this case 20px, color and background color depend on color scale being used in the page

  • Code and other syntaxis must go in the <code> tag, using & lt and & gt to show the markup on the site, on a light gray with .6 opacity to it
